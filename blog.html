Reminders for a Muslim

Maintain a discipline life:Everday as a Muslim, we have to perform Salah. We need to maintain the exact time to connect ourselves to pray in congregation. We must cycle our work through Salah. NOT to adjust with our work. Every Time when we pray timely,we can feel there is a Refreshment and peace in innner our soul. We can get back into track by performing salah in time and also lead a disciplined life by practicing other mandatories of ISLAM.
Follow Sunnah and Our Life:Sunnah is the saying of Prophet Muhammad ṣallallāhu 'alayhi wa sallam (peace and blessings of Allāh be upon him). When we start practicing the Sunnah that Our prophet teaches us, our life will become beautiful, joyful with happiness. Beacuse the Sunnahs improves our productivity as well as our personality.So, we must follow the sunnah of our Prophet Muhammad ṣallallāhu 'alayhi wa sallam (peace and blessings of Allāh be upon him).
